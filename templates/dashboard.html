JWT Privilege Escalation Demo

This tool demonstrates the JWT vulnerability by modifying your token to escalate your role from "user" to "admin".

How it works:

  1. Your JWT token is decoded (it's not encrypted!)
  2. The 'role' claim is modified from 'user' to 'admin'
  3. The token is re-signed with the weak secret key 'secret'
  4. Your session is updated with the forged token
  5. You now have admin access!
